Для подключения Samsung S8 from Voyager to Paragon.

 -  снять галку автостарта приложения Voyager:
Go to TEMS Voyager app, tap on the top right corner lines > Settings > Voyager > General settings > TEMS Pocket > uncheck "Autostart TEMS Pocket"
- Go back to the main Voyager screen and the tap again on the top-right corner line to open the menu > Exit > "Are you sure you want to exit?" . YES > "Do you want to close the back end as well?" > YES.
After that reboot the device. Make sure voyager app is not starting automatically.
If so, then connect the device to TEMS Paragon and check if it is recognized properly.

Starting and restarting the TEMS Samsung Galaxy S10
In order to boot the device using the pre-installed TEMS firmware, all models of the Samsung Galaxy S10 must be started in the following manner:
Power on by pressing Volume up + Bixby + Power button, release all buttons when the Bootloader warning is displayed: Started with TEMS firmware, full functionality
If started/restarted in the following manner users can get other results:
Tapping Restart in the phone menu: Started with Samsung original firmware, no TEMS functionality.
Power on by pressing Volume up + Bixby + Power button, keep holding all buttons when the Bootloader warning is displayed: Started in Recovery mode.
